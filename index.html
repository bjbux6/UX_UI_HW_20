Research

I started my process by conducting interviews with 5 users who plan travel on a regular basis. I wanted to gain insights on the steps that these users take when planning their travel. Through these interviews I discovered several useful trends. Through these trends I implemented solutions into the design of this mobile applications.



Problem Statement


Travelers need a more organized and consolidated method of planning and tracking travel in order to relieve the stress of planning trips.
